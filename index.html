About Me

Kalungwe Mwenshi, The Dot-com Entrepreneur, is a top-tier sales and marketing coach and business consultant. He empowers business owners and entrepreneurs to close bigger deals, build stronger brands, and scale their ventures through practical strategies that actually work.
